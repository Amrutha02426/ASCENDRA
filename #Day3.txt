Q) Describe a time when things did not go according to your 
plan/Tell us about a problem you solved creatively. 


During my second year of college, our NSS unit hosted the Trivandrum 
Regional Meet up of the NRPF unit of NSS. It was the first time our 
college was hosting such a large event, and everyone was working hard 
to ensure its success despite having limited facilities. 

Although I was not part of the executive committee, I volunteered to 
help manage the event. One of the activities planned for the afternoon 
was an outdoor seed sapling program. However, due to unexpected 
heavy rainfall, it became clear about an hour before the activity that it 
could not be conducted as planned. 

When the regional coordinators asked about a backup plan, we realized 
that none had been prepared. Instead of panicking, I quickly discussed 
alternatives with my co-volunteers. Within an hour, we arranged the 
materials needed, including chart papers and sketch pens, and 
organized a poster-making competition for the participants. 

The activity was conducted successfully, and the participants remained 
actively engaged despite the sudden change in plans. This experience 
taught me the importance of staying calm under pressure, thinking 
creatively, and working effectively as a team to solve unexpected 
problems.
